## Brief Introduction

&#8195;&#8195;I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Information Science at the **University of Chinese Academy of Sciences** (2024–2027). Prior to this, I obtained a Master of Management degree from **Henan Medical University** (Library, Information, and Archives Management, 2021–2024) and a Bachelor of Management degree from **Jinling College of Nanjing University** (Information Management and Information Systems, 2017–2021).  

&#8195;&#8195;My research primarily focuses on scientometrics, with an emphasis on scientific innovation, academic journals, open science, and science and technology evaluation. I also maintain a strong interest in peer review and research ethics, and am committed to promoting academic integrity. In addition, I am actively exploring digital humanities and smart library construction.  

&#8195;&#8195;To date, I have published over 20 academic papers. My research findings have appeared in international journals such as ***Humanities and Social Sciences Communications***, ***Journal of Medical Internet Research***, ***Scientometrics***, ***Journal of Information Science***, ***Journal of Scholarly Publishing***, and ***Sustainability***, as well as in domestic core journals including ***Library and Information Service***, ***Chinese Journal of Scientific and Technical Periodicals***, ***Acta Editologica***, ***Digital Library Forum*** and ***Journal of Library and Information Science in Agriculture***.

&#8195;&#8195;Concurrently, I serves as an **Editorial Board Member** for ***COLLNET Journal of Scientometrics and Information Management*** (ESCI, 2025–present), **Subject Editor** for ***Folia Toruniensia*** (Scopus, 2026–present), **Editorial Review Board Member** for ***Health Informatics Journal*** (SCIE, 2025–present), **Editorial Board Member** for ***IP Indian Journal of Library Science and Information Technology*** (Scopus, 2026–present), **Editorial Board Member** for ***Jurnal Kajian Informasi & Perpustakaan*** (2026–present), **Early Career Editorial Board Member** for ***Knowledge Organization*** (SSCI, 2026–present), and Scientific Committee Menber for ***Revue Des Sciences De L' Information*** (2025–present).  

&#8195;&#8195;I am also reviewers for more than sixty journals, including ***Chinese Journal of Scientific and Technical Periodicals*** (CSSCI), ***BMC Medical Research Methodology*** (SCIE), ***Health Information and Libraries Journal*** (SSCI), ***Humanities and Social Sciences Communications*** (SSCI & AHCI), ***Information Research*** (SSCI), ***JMIR Medical Education*** (SCIE), ***JMIR Medical Informatics***(SCIE), ***JMIR mHealth and uHealth*** (SCIE), ***JMIR Public Health and Surveillance*** (SCIE), ***JMIR Serious Games*** (SCIE), ***Journal of Cardiothoracic Surgery*** (SCIE), ***Journal of Economic Surveys*** (SSCI), ***Journal of Information Science*** (SCIE & SSCI), ***Journal of Librarianship & Information Science*** (SSCI), ***Journal of Medical Internet Research*** (SCIE), ***Journal of the Medical Library Association*** (SSCI), ***Learned Publishing*** (SSCI), ***Malaysian Journal of Library and Information Science*** (SSCI), ***PLOS One*** (SCIE), ***Research Evalution*** (SSCI), ***Research Integrity and Peer Review*** (SCIE & SSCI), ***Scientometrics*** (SCIE & SSCI), ***Serials Review*** (SSCI). I welcome additional editorial/review invitations in related fields.  

&#8195;&#8195;Moving forward, I will further dedicate myself to advancing information science and meta-science research from a multidisciplinary perspective, focusing on the evolution of science and technology evaluation policies, academic exchange systems, and knowledge dissemination models.
